What it catches

Three ways scams reach you. One scanner.

Link & URL Analysis

Checks domains for IP addresses, lookalike spelling, shady top-level domains, link shorteners, and brand impersonation.

Email & SMS Scanning

Flags urgency tactics, requests for OTPs or card details, fake prizes, and threatening or pressuring language.

QR Code Fraud Detection

Decodes any QR image right in your browser and runs the hidden destination through the same risk engine before you scan it with your phone.

How it works
